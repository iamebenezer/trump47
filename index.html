What wallet should I use to purchase Trump 47 tokens?

You can use any Base-compatible wallet such as MetaMask (configured for Base), Trust Wallet, or Coinbase Wallet to purchase tokens on the Base blockchain equivalent of Uniswap.
How do I find the Trump 47 token on Uniswap?

You can find the token by entering its contract address in the search bar of Uniswap or your preferred Dex. The contract address will be available at launch and posted on the website and social media channels.
What is the minimum amount of ETH (BASE) required to purchase Trump 47 tokens?

There is no minimum amount of ETH required to purchase Trump 47 tokens. You can swap any amount of ETH for Trump 47 tokens on Uniswap, subject to network gas fees.
Are there any fees associated with purchasing Trump 47 tokens on Uniswap?

Yes, there are two types of fees:

  1. Base Liquidity Provider Fee: A small fee that goes to liquidity providers on the Base network.
  2. Base Network Gas Fee: A fee required to process transactions on the Base blockchain.
